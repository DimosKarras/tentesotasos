Certificates

Our company provides high-quality awnings and military tents that meet the standards set forth by the European Union's CE certification. As a company committed to excellence, we ensure that all our products undergo rigorous testing and conform to the essential health, safety, and environmental requirements outlined in the CE directives.


we are proud to possess a NATO Cage Code, signifying our status as an authorized supplier to NATO member countries. This not only reinforces our commitment to quality and reliability but also allows us to play a significant role in supporting defense and security operations for NATO nations.


By complying with ISO 9000:2015 standards, we strive to exceed customer expectations, maintain consistency in product quality, and optimize our internal processes. This dedication to excellence empowers us to deliver reliable, safe, and innovative awnings, military tents, and related solutions to our customers worldwide.


The company is certified by the General Secretariat of Economic Planning and Defense Investments for its ability to manufacture tents and covers for vehicles, armored combat vehicles, and weapons of the Armed Forces.
